Vaccinations by County

This interactive map depicts administered vaccinations in each county, and then further differentiates the number of doses the individual recieved, as well as their age and whether the area is considered a metro or non-metro county. Additionally, the map also allows the user to see the distribution of vaccines from 2021 to date.

Doses Administered and Vaccinated Status by County

The interactive line graph above depictes the amount of doses administered against the number of fully vaccinated individuals. The data is visualized longitudinally from January 2021 to present day.

Longitudinal Vaccination Trend

The interactive line graph above represents vaccinations administered by county, the ages of the indivuduals who recieved the vaccine as well as if was administered in a metro or non-metro area. Similarily to the above, the data is January 2021 to present day.
